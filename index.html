About SUGL

The Scottish University Gymnastics League (SUGL) is focused on running competitions and events for university and adult gymnastics in Scotland. We bring together adults from universities and clubs across the country to compete, train, and celebrate the sport of gymnastics.

Our mission is to support adult gymnastics in Scotland, providing competitive opportunities, and fostering an inclusive community.
