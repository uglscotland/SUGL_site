Judging Course

Essential judging certification for university gymnastics competitions

October 18-19, 2025
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Stirling

Course Overview

This comprehensive two-day judging course is designed to provide participants with the essential knowledge and skills required to officiate at university gymnastics competitions. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, this course will prepare you for the upcoming competition season.

Prerequisites

No prior judging experience required. Open to all university students interested in gymnastics. Basic understanding of gymnastics terminology is helpful but not essential.

Course Modules

Day 1: Fundamentals of Gymnastics Judging

  • Introduction to gymnastics scoring systems
  • Understanding difficulty and execution scores
  • Basic judging terminology and hand signals
  • Equipment and apparatus overview
  • Safety protocols and regulations

Day 2: Advanced Judging Techniques

  • Detailed scoring criteria for each apparatus
  • Common deductions and penalties
  • Judging panel coordination
  • Video review and analysis
  • Practical judging exercises

What You'll Learn

  • How to evaluate gymnastics routines objectively
  • Understanding of FIG (International Gymnastics Federation) rules
  • Proper scoring methodology and documentation
  • Communication skills for judging panels
  • Handling challenging judging situations
  • Preparation for official competition judging

Course Schedule

Saturday, October 18th

9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Theory and fundamentals
1:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Practical exercises and demonstrations

Sunday, October 19th

9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Advanced techniques and scoring
1:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Final assessments and certification
4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Q&A and course wrap-up

Materials Provided

  • Comprehensive judging manual
  • Scoring sheets and templates
  • Video examples and case studies
  • Certificate upon completion
  • Access to online resources

Official Certification

Receive SUGL-recognized judging certification upon successful completion
